# Cops and Robbers

Cops and Robbers adds a small playable police-and-bank scenario to Minecraft worlds.

Build a police station, place a bank, patrol in a police cruiser, and chase down robbers before they make off with stolen gold. The mod is designed for family worlds where kids want a clear, playful objective without needing a large quest system.

## Features

- Rideable police cruiser with lights, siren, controller-friendly shortcuts, and creative-mode flight tricks.
- Rideable fire truck with a larger vehicle profile for emergency scenes.
- Bank robbers that target nearby banks and can scatter when patrol vehicles approach.
- Police cruiser capture behavior for collecting robbers and dropping them near jail cells.
- Placeable police station kit with a jail cell and cruiser garage.
- Placeable bank kit with tellers, a vault, and robber targets.
- Creative spawn eggs for cruisers, fire trucks, cops, robbers, bank tellers, and firefighters.

## Why Cops and Robbers?

This is a lightweight pretend-play mod for family Minecraft sessions. It gives kids a reason to patrol, chase, rescue, and rebuild while keeping the systems simple enough to understand from the first few minutes.

## Requirements

Cops and Robbers must be installed on every participating client and on the server or LAN host.

- Minecraft Java Edition
- Fabric Loader
- Fabric API
